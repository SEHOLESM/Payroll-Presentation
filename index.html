Payroll & Leave Process Overview

Presented by: Sihle Sehole

About Our Payroll System

We use PaySpace to process payroll. Salaries are paid on the last day of each month. The payroll cycle begins on the 26th and ends on the 25th of the following month.

If you are a new employee, your first salary will be pro-rated based on the number of days worked.

Salary Calculation Example

If your monthly earning is R3000 and the standard month is 21.67 working days:

  • Daily rate: R3000 ÷ 21.67 = R138.44
  • If you worked 20 days: 138.44 × 20 = R2768.80

Query and Payslip Policy

  • Payslips are uploaded on ESS (Employee Self Service).
  • Queries must be sent via your manager only.
  • Do not email payroll directly – queries will be ignored.
  • Late queries = handled in the next month.

Leave Policy & ESS Usage

  • Apply for leave on ESS.
  • Add doctor’s notes and comments.
  • Best practice: Talk to your manager before applying.
  • Leave remains pending until approved.
  • After 20 working days, your leave balance starts accruing.
  • To accrue 1 full day of annual leave (on a Monday–Friday work schedule), you must work approximately 17 to 18 days in the month.

Banking & Shift Allowance

  • Submit a copy of your new bank Account and Complite Change of Banking Details form.
  • Shift allowance = 6% of hourly rate.
  • Example: R3000 → R138.44 daily → R17.31 hourly → 6% = R1.04 per hour
  • Worked 4.75 hours in a shift = R4.93 extra earning

Commission Process

Commissions are calculated and added to your payslip the following month.

Final Notes

  • Always check the group chat if you're on leave or absent.
  • Managers will prioritize absent employees for important updates.
  • Ask your manager if you have any questions.
